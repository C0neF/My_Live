# Product

## Register

product

## Users

My Live users watch live streams on Android phones and TV-style devices. They are usually browsing, entering rooms, leaving rooms, switching platforms, and managing playback settings.

## Product Purpose

The app gives users a direct way to browse supported live platforms and watch rooms with stable playback, readable chat, and predictable navigation.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, direct, practical.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative motion, slow page choreography, heavy visual effects, and inconsistent navigation behavior that interrupts playback workflows.

## Design Principles

- Keep motion short and state-driven.
- Prioritize frame stability during playback-adjacent navigation.
- Preserve familiar Android and Compose interaction patterns.
- Keep route behavior consistent unless a screen has clear performance needs.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Motion should stay restrained and avoid unnecessary choreography. UI changes should preserve standard accessibility labels and predictable back behavior.
